Our Story Of Genesis

Amid the deadly corona pandemic, when the entire world was engulfed with the fear of stepping out of their home, a 18 year old boy hailing from one of the backward and violence affected districts in Bihar was using his education as a tool to serve the affected mankind. He was serving the underprivileged communities who were not getting benefited by any govt schemes, with relief materials in the deep forest villages in Jamui district of Bihar. Jamui is one among the most backward districts in the country and was heavily affected by violence of naxals for so long. In 2006 the Ministry of Panchayati Raj named Jamui one of the country‚Äôs 250 most backward districts (out of a total of 640). Jamui is also included in the list of aspirational districts which are most developmentally challenged districts across sectors such as health and nutrition; education; agriculture and water resources; basic infrastructure; and financial inclusion and skill development.

Used and Repaired clothes, shoes, books and other usable things for the underprivileged people served by our target audience i.e. select school students, serving the cause of zero waste management and developing social consciousness among the GenX. This is done to engage with the fellows and make them familiar with our future course of program

Spreading awarenes about ISR

ISR or individual social responsibility refers to our awareness of how our actions affect the community as a whole. ISR can include volunteering time, giving money, and standing up for issues that affect the rights of others.

Localisation of SDGs

Educating youth about SDGs and sensitizing them to act upon this through an experiential journey of learning, changemaking and leadership so that they can use their education, creativity and skills as a tool to change the world.
