About Us

YSpark is a youth-led voluntary social initiative which aims at sparking (sensitizing) youth for catalyzing change by cultivating social conscience and localisation of SDG’s among them at school level. We are up to engaging the GenX at school level, building young cohorts who care about the issues that matter to the generation, nurturing an ecosystem towards a sustainably developed world around us.

YSPARK's Vision: To creat leaders in every state.

Our Vision

It’s an action oriented Leadership Development Program based on developing social conscience at School Level for the GenX, driving grassroots impact in line with the UN’s SDGs. unite like minded people who can contribute in the transition of INDIA from a developing country to a developed country.

Recycle. Reduce. Reuse

Used and Repaired clothes, shoes, books and other usable things for the underprivileged people served by our target audience i.e. select school students, serving the cause of zero waste management and developing social consciousness among the GenX. This is done to engage with the fellows and make them familiar with our future course of program

Educating the young ones

Educating youth about SDGs and sensitizing them to act upon this through an experiential journey of learning, changemaking and leadership so that they can use their education as a tool to change the world.



But Why ?

As rightly said by Nelson Mandela, “The Youth of today are the leaders of tomorrow”, and in future they’ll be leading their own respective domains. It is important to develop social conscience among youth from an early age because when they’ll be leading their respective domains in future, they’ll be conscious about the social issues and problems, and they will make decisions and take actions keeping in mind about the social issues and how their actions can have impact on the life of people. Change is our collective responsibility and we can address our responsibility if we will build social conscience among youth from an early age.
